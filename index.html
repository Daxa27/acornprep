About Us


Acorn Prep paves a simple route for struggling SAT test-takers. Our tutoring service allows you to communicate with top students across the globe for personal consultation and advice. Given that our services are provided by students, we can guarantee a warm, embracing community for you to rely on at all times. Acorn prep unlocks a haven of resources where you can find a variety of popular books and plenty of practice materials. At Acorn Prep we believe that money shouldn't hold you back, so the best part of the experience is that we provide 100% need-blind services! So what are you waiting for? Sign up now and start your journey with Acorn Prep.



Our Mission

AcornPrep was created with the vision of providing universal access to high quality tutoring for big international exams.

Universal Access

We provide students who cannot afford tutors with free education so that they face no boundaries in their journey.

Our Tutors

We provide the best student tutors with experience to help you in every step towards your dream scores.



Our Services


Tutoring

We conduct one-on-one as well as group sessions on the platform of your choice (eg: Zoom). Our learning model focuses on teaching to the test with first hand experience from our tutors.

Resources

We suggest and provide the best resources which have proven to work for top scorers. We also have a private Discord Server for students to get instant answers and help.

Counseling

As students, we understand that you face issues with learning and guidance and so we are also available to talk to anytime regarding your prep, school work and other academic issues.



Why Choose Us?


Firsthand Experience

Our tutors have firsthand experience of studying for and taking the tests and are in the 99+ percentile of their fields. They also have experience of juggling schoolwork and test prep and therefore understand difficulties students face.

Worldwide Service

Our tutors are located in multiple timezones and as a result, we are able to cover most parts of the globe. We are available at multiple hours during the day and at night for tutoring and questions (on our Discord Server).
